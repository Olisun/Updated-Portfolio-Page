About Me

I retired after 20 year career in financial services in the middle of 2015. I've been on a personal sabbatical for the last four years. During this time, I started and operated my own business. A dog jogging and dog walking walking business. For a while, I was pretty successful, mainly because I think I was the only dog walker in San Francisco who offered a service of running dogs. Unfortunately, however, I got a stress fracture in my leg from running so much and had to put my business on hold for 8 months. When I went back to my dog running business, I had to hold back literally from the mileage as per instructed by my orthopedic doctors. Needless to say I had to pivot.

I've always been a tech product geek. It wasn't until March-April of this year that I really started looking into how things worked under the hood. One thing led to another and now I'm currently enrolled in a coding boot camp in San Francisco. I'm looking to start a new chapter in my life with a second career as a software developer and at the age of 47 (almost 48) and after not working in an office for over four years. What are the odds? Life is not exciting to me if you don't have some kind of extreme challenge to conquer and I have a big one in front of me.
